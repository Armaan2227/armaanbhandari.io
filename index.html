About Me

Welcome to the personal homepage of Armaan Bhandari, an Integrated Dual Degree (IDD) student in Mechanical Engineering at IIT (BHU) Varanasi, expected to graduate in 2027.

My work spans robotics, control systems design, embedded systems, and autonomous vehicles. I am particularly interested in the dynamics and control of aerial robots, hybrid locomotion systems, and the integration of hardware and software for real-world autonomous operation.

I spent Summer 2025 as a research intern at the DISCO Lab, IIT Kanpur, working under Prof. Abilash Patel on morphing amphibious drones. I have published a paper at the 11th Indian Control Conference (ICC-11) at IISc Bengaluru.
